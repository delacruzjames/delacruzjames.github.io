About me

I'm a software developer from Manila, Philippines. I have 5 years of software development experience mostly from software company startups. My core technical skills are Ruby on Rails, AngualarJS/ JavaScript, Linux, MySQL, and PostgreSQL. I describe myself as a full-stack web developer, and I also have a working knowledge in iOS and Android development using xcode/swift and as/java.

I'm also 2nd dan Blackbelter from Kyokushin Karate Organization. I fought in 9th and 10th World Kyokushin Tournament held in Japan. I was awarded All Asia Best Technique 2012. and Top 6 Best Fighter in All Asia.

I have two kids already and a lovely wife. I used to work remotely for 2 years and spend much time to my family while I am earning. I love to learn new stuff and new technologies.
